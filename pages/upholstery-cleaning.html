Overview

Designed for clients who want a clear service scope and premium-level follow-through.

Upholstery Cleaning helps restore a fresher, more cared-for appearance to the furniture clients use every day. It is ideal for family seating, lounge spaces, and homes with pets.

When clients usually book this service

  • A strong finishing touch for living rooms, dens, and waiting areas
Benefits
